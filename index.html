AF-MPD Thruster with Electrostatic TVC
July 2023 — June 2024 · M.E. Thesis

Led development of a hybrid Air-Breathing Magneto-Plasma-Dynamic (AF-MPD) thruster with dual ion acceleration stages and integrated electrostatic thrust vector control via a segmented anode design. Conducted experimental testing and plasma diagnostics in VLEO conditions.

Multistage Gridded Ion Thruster
Mar 2023 — June 2023 · M.E. 2nd Sem

Designed and fabricated a multistage ion thruster grid configuration maximizing ionization degree and minimizing internal drag using an Aeroelectrodynamic Honeycomb Grid Structure. Prototyped via SolidWorks and 3D printing, followed by experimental validation.

+12.6% thrust · −8.7% parasitic drag vs. conventional grid
✈️
Boundary Layer Suction on Wingtips
Oct 2021 — May 2022 · B.E. Thesis

Designed and simulated an airfoil prototype equipped with active Boundary Layer Suction (BLS) technology to reduce wingtip vortex-induced drag. Used SolidWorks and CATIA for design, ANSYS Fluent for CFD analysis.

−19.08% induced drag via active flow control
